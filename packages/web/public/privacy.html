Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

ClankyBuddy is a browser game. It runs entirely on your device. This policy describes what that means for your privacy as it works today.

What we collect

Nothing. ClankyBuddy does not have user accounts, does not ask for your email, name, or any personal information, and does not send your activity to any server. There is no analytics tracking and no advertising.

What's stored on your device

Your game progress — currency, unlocked tools, and settings — is saved in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device. We cannot see it. Clearing your browser data, or using the in-game reset, erases it permanently.

Hosting

The site is served as static files by Cloudflare. Like any website, Cloudflare's edge network may process standard request metadata (such as IP address) to deliver the page and protect against abuse, governed by Cloudflare's privacy policy. We do not receive, store, or have access to this information.

Fonts

The page loads the Inter typeface from rsms.me. Your browser contacts that host to fetch the font files.

Children

ClankyBuddy depicts cartoon violence and is intended for users aged 13 and over.

Changes

If we add features that collect data — such as a global leaderboard or accounts — we will update this policy before those features go live, and this page will describe exactly what changes.

This is a hobby project. If you have questions, the source is open on GitHub.
